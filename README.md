# 📊 Meta Ad Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes advertising performance data from Meta platforms (Facebook & Instagram) to evaluate campaign effectiveness, audience engagement, and conversion performance.

The analysis focuses on identifying key performance drivers and optimizing marketing strategies using data-driven insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Objective

The objective of this project is to:

* Track performance of ad campaigns across platforms
* Measure key KPIs such as CTR, Engagement Rate, and Conversion Rate
* Identify high-performing audience segments
* Optimize budget allocation and ad strategy

---

## 📂 Dataset Description

The dataset is structured into four relational tables:

* **ad_events** → User interactions (Impressions, Clicks, Shares, Comments, Purchases)
* **ads** → Ad details (platform, ad type, targeting)
* **campaigns** → Campaign-level budget and duration
* **users** → User demographics and geographic information

## 📈 Key Insights

* High CTR (~11.7%) indicates strong ad creatives and targeting
* Engagement Rate (~13.5%) shows users actively interact with ads
* Low Purchase Rate (~0.6%) highlights inefficiencies in the conversion funnel
* Female users and age group 18–30 generate the highest engagement
* Video and Story ads outperform Image and Carousel formats
* Peak engagement occurs during afternoon and evening hours
* India and US are top-performing regions in terms of engagement

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Improve landing pages and conversion funnel to increase purchase rate
* Focus targeting on high-performing segments (Females, 18–30)
* Allocate more budget to Video and Story ads
* Schedule campaigns during peak engagement hours
* Optimize campaigns for high-performing geographies
